Lessons in Electric Circuits

Six free textbooks on electricity and electronics, from fundamentals to experiments. By Tony R. Kuphaldt. Works offline.

Vol. I DC Ohm's law, series and parallel circuits, capacitance, magnetism, and network analysis. Vol. II AC Phasors, reactance, impedance, resonance, filters, transformers, and AC motors. Vol. III Semiconductors Diodes, bipolar transistors, FETs, thyristors, and operational amplifiers. Vol. IV Digital Logic gates, Boolean algebra, Karnaugh maps, flip-flops, counters, and shift registers. Vol. V Reference Conversion tables, formula sheets, schematic symbols, and the periodic table. Vol. VI Experiments Hands-on bench experiments for DC, AC, semiconductor, and digital circuits.
